Who are we?

Wameedh is a scientific club founded by IGEE Students (ex INELEC) in 2016. We aim to learn, practice and innovate in the fields of electronics and new technologies. We focus on developing technical skills, we offer many workshops in the field of Electronics and Electrical Engineering (Robotics, Embedded Systems, AI...).
